International Packing Symbols

1. When you see two hands cupping a cube, you know that you should “handle with care.” Contents are more than likely fragile, made up of intricate parts or otherwise easily damaged

2. The image of two upward arrows above a box indicates, “This way up.” For the duration of shipping/delivery, the carton should face upright

3. This cracked wine glass label lets you know the item(s) inside is “fragile.” You’ll notice this symbol on cartons containing items such as china and glassware

4. The umbrella reminds you to “keep dry.” The package should never get wet or be stored in damp areas
